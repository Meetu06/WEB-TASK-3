Best Consultancy For You

Mr J D Pawar is an Electrical Engineering graduate from Pune University. More than 25 years of Rich, diversified industrial experience mainly in Technical Design & Project Management. Almost Ten years are in French MNC – Saint Gobain Sekurit an Automotive Glass division of Safety glass, Toughened & Laminated both as Head – Product Development / Internal Projects. J D Pawar has successfully executed automotive glass development projects for major automotive OEM’s on National & International level, like Toyota, Hyundai, Suzuki, GM, Tata Motors, Ford, Fiat, Mahindra, Renault, Volvo, Bajaj Auto, PVPL etc. Since 2009, J D Pawar is working as an Independent Technical consultant & providing project design services to architectural/ OEM/ Automotive safety glass processing plants. Certified Internal Quality Auditor for ISO, QS-9000, TS – 16949, by TUV. Hands on experience for all types of machineries for installation and commissioning.
